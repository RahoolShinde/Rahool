My Best Series of All time

Hollywood

1. Game Of Thrones

It keeps you engaged throughout the season and you never gets bored watching it. All the characters just make you beleive in them. Talking about direction, cinematography, story telling, writing is absolutely exceptional.

2. Vikings

It also keeps you engaged as the story progresses. History of Viking Army and every one who stood up to believe in their God make it more intense at same time interesting to watch. It shows great bond between brothers though they are step brothers.

3. Lucifer

The very first episode blows your mind by acting skills of Lucifer himself. The concept of series quite unique which shows great balance of Good and Bad things.
